# Meridian Finance

### Digital Lending & Financial Solutions

Meridian Finance is a financial technology company operating under **Meridian Capital**, focused on delivering innovative salary advance and consumer lending solutions through modern digital platforms.

# Project Meridian

## Product

### Meridian Lending Platform

A digital salary advance lending platform designed to streamline loan origination, approval workflows, and document processing through modern fintech technologies.

---

## Mission

To simplify access to short-term financing while improving operational efficiency through automation, transparency, and intelligent document processing.
